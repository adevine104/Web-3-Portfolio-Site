PUBLICATION

BACKTALK

I designed a print magazine from the ground up as a project for my publication class. I started with a concept that I thought up and a borrowed article from The Atlantic. I then created the masthead, refined the idea behind the magazine, worked on the imagery and got started building my creation.

BACKTALK is a Monthly commentary magazine for people 18+ that focuses on thoughtprovoking articles and imagery. It features a broad range of social, cultural and even scientific topics. It reveals ideas for those who want to see the world from new and different perspectives.
